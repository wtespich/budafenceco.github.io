About Buda Fence Company

Your trusted fence installation and repair experts serving Austin, Buda, and Central Texas since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Buda Fence Company began with a simple mission: to provide Central Texas homeowners with high-quality fencing solutions that combine durability, beauty, and value. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted fence installation companies in the Austin area.

Over the years, we've completed more than 5,000 fence installations, earning a reputation for exceptional craftsmanship, reliable service, and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to quality has made us the go-to choice for homeowners throughout Buda, Austin, Kyle, and Dripping Springs.

Today, we continue to uphold the same values that founded our company: integrity, quality workmanship, and putting our customers first in everything we do.
